FindMyMidwife

The place where moms and midwifes find each other in a stress free and efficient way. The mom (or soon to be mom) can set a filter of the specialty she is looking for in a midwife and receive only suggestions that are suitable for her (midwifes that work in the area she lives and that have availability to assist the mom when she needs it).

is also a work tool for midwifes, where they have everything they need in one place: making notes on the patient's profile, make appointments for them, have a calendar overview of all their appointments, address and important information. Also a chat feature so that they don't have to use their private number for work!
